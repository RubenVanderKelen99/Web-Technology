Soon this will be your space

Posted on Friday March 21, 2014 by davy.dewinne

This blog will be the space to share your work & experiences!

At the beginning of Mind The Gap you will be divided in teams. Each team gets an account to use this blog.
With this account you can login to the administrator part

Every post can exist out of:

  • Title (required)
  • Introduction (like the first italic sentence of this post)
  • Highlighted image
  • Body with different paragraphs, images, links, ...
  • Video, a link to youtube, vimeo or slideshare, automatically included in the post

Before you can add pictures, you have to upload them first. You can do this at the Administrator page > Settings > Uploaded files

Have fun!
